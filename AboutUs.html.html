About Us

Our Mission

At ADAPT, our mission is to redefine your smartphone experience. We believe in a world where technology adapts to your needs, providing flexibility, sustainability, and unmatched performance. Our modular phones empower you to tailor your device to match your lifestyle seamlessly.

Student-Driven Innovation

As first-year students at the University of Ottawa, we bring fresh perspectives and boundless creativity to the tech industry. We understand the dynamic needs of today's users and strive to introduce innovative solutions that keep you ahead of the curve.

Modular Excellence

Our phones are not just devices; they're an ecosystem. Experience the freedom to customize and upgrade specific features, ensuring your device grows with you. From enhanced cameras to extended battery life, ADAPT gives you the power to choose.

Sustainability Matters

We are committed to a sustainable future. ADAPT modular phones are designed with eco-friendly materials and a focus on reducing electronic waste. Join us in making responsible choices for the planet while enjoying state-of-the-art technology.
